Awards & Recognition

Celebrating excellence in integrative oncology research and clinical presentations at SANGAMA 2026.

🏆 Prizes for Postgraduate Students Only 🏆

All prizes are awarded ONLY to postgraduate students (MD, MS, MDS, BAMS, BNYS, BUMS, BHMS, PhD scholars). Faculty and senior researchers are eligible to present but are not eligible for prizes.
Award Category Cash Prize Trophy + Certificate
Best Oral Paper — 1st Prize PG only ₹15,000 Gold Trophy
Best Oral Paper — 2nd Prize PG only ₹10,000 Silver Trophy
Best Oral Paper — 3rd Prize PG only ₹5,000 Bronze Trophy
Best Poster — 1st Prize PG only ₹10,000 Gold Trophy
Best Poster — 2nd Prize PG only ₹7,000 Silver Trophy
Best Poster — 3rd Prize PG only ₹3,000 Bronze Trophy
Young Researcher Award (Best Overall PG Presentation) ₹20,000 Special Trophy
Integration Award (Best Cross-System Research — PG only) ₹10,000 Special Trophy
Total Prize Pool: ₹80,000 + trophies and certificates

Prizes will be distributed by the Chief Guest during the Valedictory Session on Day 2. Winners will be announced based on judges' scores during the presentations.
