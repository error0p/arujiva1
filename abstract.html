Submission Guidelines

  • 1 Deadline: Abstracts must be submitted at least 4 weeks before the symposium. Late submissions will not be accepted.
  • 2 Format: Use the structured abstract format — Title, Background, Objective, Methods, Results, Conclusion — within a maximum of 300 words (title and author names excluded).
  • 3 Eligible Topics: Clinical research, basic science, case studies, and systematic reviews are all welcome — across any system of medicine relevant to integrative oncology.
  • 4 Selection: Top 20 abstracts will be selected for oral presentation (10 min talk + 2 min Q&A). The next 30–40 abstracts will be selected for poster presentation (A1 size, portrait orientation).
  • 5 Registration Mandatory: All presenting authors must be registered and have paid the applicable registration fee before presenting. Register here →
  • 6 Full Paper (Optional): Authors may optionally submit a full paper for inclusion in the symposium proceedings booklet. Upload your full paper (PDF/DOCX, max 5 MB) in the form below.
  • 7 Language: All submissions must be in English. The Scientific Committee's decision on selection is final.

Judging Criteria
